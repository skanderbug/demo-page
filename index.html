Welcome to The 2026 joint meeting of RMT and SNAB

The 2026 joint meeting of RMT and SNAB (Statistical Network Analysis and Beyond) will take place on June 2-4, 2026 at the renowned Hilton Nanjing Niushoushan in Nanjing, China.

About the Conference

The 2026 joint meeting of RMT and SNAB aims to create a dynamic forum for researchers in the field of statistical network analysis, random matrix theory, and related areas to exchange ideas, discuss cutting-edge research, and explore future directions in this rapidly evolving discipline.

Topics Include:

SNAB

  • Network data modeling and analysis
  • Dynamical network modeling
  • AI assisted inference & prediction
  • Tensor data analysis
  • Text analysis
  • Unsupervised learning
  • Transfer learning
  • Privacy analysis

RMT

  • Random matrix theory
  • Free probably theory
  • Spiked model analysis
  • Large dimensional statistical analysis
  • Heavy-tailed data analysis
  • Modern Signal Processing
  • Statistical Physics
  • Large-scale Machine Learning
